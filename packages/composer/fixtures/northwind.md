Sales dashboard for Northwind, a B2B SaaS company.

Northwind sells enterprise software with 6-month sales cycles. Reps focus on
champion identification and MEDDIC qualification.

Home page (Pipeline) shows:
- Deal pipeline kanban filtered to active stages (discovery, qualified, proposal, negotiation)
- Activity feed below showing recent team activity
- Rep leaderboard on the right showing top reps by deal value

Clicking a deal opens a detail view in a sidebar showing deal info, contacts,
activities, and quick actions: log activity, move stage, close deal.

Leads page shows the lead list with default filter "qualified". Each row has
a "Convert to deal" button.
